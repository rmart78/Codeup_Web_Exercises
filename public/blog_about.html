About Raul Martinez

A native of San Antonio, Texas, few knew that one day Raul would rise to prominence in the world of front-end web development. Although fulfilled by his work, he found himself requiring a creative outlet through which he could voice his many, many opinions on a wide range of issues. Our of this yearning in his soul rose the Rock Salt blog, a place where Raul could tell it like it is without fear of reprisals or contradicting opinions.

Raul invites you to comment often.
